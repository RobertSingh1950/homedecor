Coasters: A Versatile and Useful Item

The Need for Coasters and their Purpose

Coasters as Drink Holders

Of course, the most common use for a coaster is to protect your furniture from water rings and scratches. When you place a drink on a coaster, the liquid is absorbed by the coaster instead of your furniture. This helps to prevent water rings and scratches, and it also helps to keep your furniture looking its best.

Types of Coasters
